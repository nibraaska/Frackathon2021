Frackathon 2021-2022

A JAM'ENG Event

Introduction

In the quaint town of New Orleans in Louisiana, a yearly conference of the JAM'ENG friend group shall take place. The titans of industry and academia shall clash for the opportunity to be voted the best project of the summit.

Along with such a ground breaking conference, we shall enjoy the sights of New Orleans and enjoy the city on New Years night. Prepare yourself for a weekend of elegance and beautiful code.

Please tell your friends about this event. See below for the kinds of projects we are open to!

Projects

Transportation

JAM'ENG members from all over the globe plan on attending this momentous conference. Due to nature of member locations, we plan on converging in New Orleans with the use of Airplanes. If this doesn't work for you, it should be noted that New Orleans is around a 9 hour drive from Nashville, TN.

Accommodations

We plan on indulging slightly with a large Airbnb in the heart of the city. Since all members of JAM'ENG, other than those who are still students, have full time jobs, we plan on enjoying ourselves. It should be noted that all expenses will be reasonable and not unaffordable.

City Life

We are aware that not all members of JAM'ENG and guests will want to spend the entire time working on their projects. The location of the event allows for members to take a small step outside of the living space and enjoy the beautiful city of New Orleans. This event is as much about enjoyment as it is about coding.

Projects

This event is open to all projects. We don't descrimate against software developlment, hardware, artificial intelligence, machine learning, or any kind of technology or tools. Feel free to be open and express yourself as the person you are during this event. See below for some idea but we are open for anything!
